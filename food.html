Hungry? Try these!

Odette

Odette is a neighborhood eatery and shop in historic downtown Florence, Alabama serving elevated American fare with southern and international influences. The menu features locally- and sustainably-sourced ingredients in a contemporary, inviting and comfortable atmosphere.

Rattlesnake Saloon

A watering hole under the rock, this is a place the whole family can come relax, connect to local history, fill their bellies and listen to live, local music. The setting is in the shadows of a local cave and is truly a unique experience. Their Duke Rustler burger is named one of Alabama's top burgers by the Alabama Cattlemen's Association and a public opinion poll.

The Salty Cod

The Salty Cod is a locally owned and operated English style Pub located just off the historic downtown square in Athens, Alabama. We maintain a British/European draft selection as well as a variety of bottled beers, ales and ciders from across Europe and USA. Our food menu focuses primarily on English and Irish fare, while also offering a Gelato, coffee and pastry shop as well as a full Butcher shop. Our pub will make you feel like you are across the pond.

Rosie's Mexican Cantina

Rosie's Mexican Cantina serves traditional Tex-Mex cuisine in a festive atmosphere and boasts several locations in Huntsville, along with a location in Florence, Alabama. In addition to its reputation for serving breast meat chicken and homemade tortillas, Rosie's is known for its special house and vegetarian dishes.The homemade chips and salsa are some of the best around, and Rosie's menu offers plenty of variety to satisfy all of your Tex-Mex cravings.

360 Grille

The 360 Grille is aptly named: This sky-high restaurant slowly revolves, giving diners a panoramic and breathtaking view of the charming town of Florence. Not only are the views stunning, but the kitchen at the 360 Grille also prepares elegant dishes full of unique flavor. Soak in the sights as you indulge in one of our signature cocktails, an expertly prepared steak, or a decadent dessert that is perfect for sharing.

Trowbridge's Ice Cream and Sandwich Shop

Trowbridge's is the oldest business still operating in its original location in Florence. It opened in 1918 and is still owned by the Trowbridge family. The family is known for dishing out classic soda fountain fare and churning up some of the area's best ice cream. The menu is full of classic soda fountain snacks such as hot dogs and sandwiches, but the signature orange pineapple ice cream is unforgettable.

Wildflower Cafe

Part restaurant, part retail store, Mentone's Wildflower Cafe strives to be environmentally conscious and health conscious too. The restaurant uses local, organic and hormone-free ingredients whenever possible, but that doesn't mean that the menu is free of indulgences. Be sure to stop in on the weekend for live music and shop the café's country store, where you'll find gourmet coffee, foods, gifts and local art.

The Bottle

The Bottle, an upscale eatery that features Southern Cuisine with a French influence, specializes in steaks, chops and a variety of the freshest fish available. The changing seasonal menu includes raw oysters, jumbo lump Maryland style crab cakes, veal chops, lamb racks and Hereford Choice Beef. The Bottle - a name referencing the near mythic, long-shuttered Green Bottle Grill - invites guests an experience in southern hospitality that will not soon be forgotten.

Pizzelle's Confections

Pizzelle's Confections is an eclectic mix of edible art and culinary exploration. We bring our lifelong love of fine chocolate, candies, and art to every creation by using fine, fair-trade chocolate and as many local, seasonal ingredients as possible. Our mission is to share our love and knowledge of chocolate and confections so that our customers have a greater understanding and appreciation of fine chocolate and confectionery art.

Cotton Row Restaurant

Chef James Boyce's Cotton Row Restaurant is housed in a historic Huntsville building constructed in 1821. Boyce opened Cotton Row in 2008 after working in cities like New York and Las Vegas. Seasonal and local ingredients dictate menu items, including the restaurant's surprisingly simple dessert, peanut butter and jelly in phyllo.

Ol' Heidelberg Cafe

Ol' Heidelberg has been serving German food since 1972 and pleasing customers for just as long. From bratwurst to red cabbage and schnitzel, the menu is filled with traditional favorites plus some more obscure fare. Ol' Heidelberg fans who once lived in Germany swear that the restaurant is as authentic as it gets.

Blue Plate Cafe

We opened in September 2003 but our story began long before that. Back when the only food sources were simple grains, dried beans, chicken, pork and summer vegetables women learned to season and flavor these with the things they had available, usually salt, pork and a few spices. We want everything, the food and especially the way you are served, to remind you of being home at Mama's kitchen table.
