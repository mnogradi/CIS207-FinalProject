Frequently Asked Questions

Question: How big is North Alabama?
Answer: Pretty big! North Alabama is made up of these counties: Blount, Cherokee, Colbert, Cullman, DeKalb, Etowah, Franklin, Jackson, Lauderdale, Lawrence, Limestone, Madison, Marion, Marshall, Morgan, Winston
Question: What is the largest city in North Alabama?
Answer: Huntsville is the largest city in North Alabama.
Question: What is the state nickname?
Answer: The nickname for Alabama is 'Heart of Dixie'.
Question: Why is Huntsville known for?
Answer: The U. S. Space and Rocket Center. It isn't called the "Rocket City" for nothing!
