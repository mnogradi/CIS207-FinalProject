FAQ

How big is North Alabama?

Pretty big! North Alabama is made up of these counties: Blount, Cherokee, Colbert, Cullman, DeKalb, Etowah, Franklin, Jackson, Lauderdale, Lawrence, Limestone, Madison, Marion, Marshall, Morgan, Winston


What's the largest city in North Alabama?

Huntsville, Alabama is the largest city in North Alabama.


What is the state nickname?

The nickname for Alabama is 'Heart of Dixie'.


Why is Huntsville known for?

The U. S. Space and Rocket Center. It isn't called the "Rocket City" for nothing!
